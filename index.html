Hi, I'm Josh, and I'm
a Father a Husband a Musician an Artist a Web Developer

Originally from Moline Illinois I moved to Kansas City to attend the Kansas City Art Institute, moving onto the University of Missouri to finish a degree in Art Thoery and Criticism.

After School I settled into the service industry where I managed a local restaurant and wine bar outside the plaza in midtown Kansas City. Spending freetime at home and school learning and writing code for various kinds of applications, my previous work and academic experience make me highly adaptable in all kinds of environments, proficient in working with front end, back end and database technologies. Using a strong foundation in basic programming languages makes me quick to learn new tech as they are rapidly evolving.
