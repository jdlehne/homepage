Where I've Been

Originally from Moline Illinois I moved to KCMO after high school to attend the Kansas City Art Institute, I moved onto the University of Missouri to finish a degree in Art Thoery and Criticism. Next up was the service industry where I managed a local staple and internationally reknowned bar in the heart of the plaza. Through all my studies and work, amidst my family, art and music, my downtime was spent persuing writing code. Though all my life experiences I've learned to be highly adaptable in all kinds of environment and proficient in Javascript, HTML5, CSS, APIs, GET and POST, Node and database technologies. Having a strong foundation in basic programming languages makes me quick to learn new tech.
