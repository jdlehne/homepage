Where I've Been

Originally from Moline Illinois I moved to KCMO right out of high school to attend the Kansas City Art Institute, then onto UMKC to finish a degree in Art Thoery and Criticism. I then moved into the service industry where I managed a local staple and internationally reknowned bar in the heart of the plaza. Through all those adventures and amidst my family, art and music my downtime was spent persuing another passion, coding. Though all my life experiences I've learned to be highly adaptable in the all kinds of environments, proficient in Javascript, HTML5, CSS, Node and database technologies. strong foundation in basic languages help me to keep learning new ones on the fly.
